Some of my favorite plants!

Hoya

Hoya are easy to grow. Low maintenance tropical plants. They bloom with a variety of scented flowers depending on species.

Philodendron

Philodendron is a variety with a vast array of leaf types. If you like variety but want to stick to one easy to care for species, you want a Philo.

Monstera

Monstra is a fruit bearing epiphyte, meaning it grows on other plants without taking nutrients. These plants are commonly grown on moss poles indoors. Easy to manage once established.
